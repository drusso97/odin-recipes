Rice Bowl with Black Beans and Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tbsp. paprika
  • 1 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp. cracked black pepper
  • 1 red onion (sliced)
  • 2 cans of chickpeas -- or 1.5 cups dry
  • 2 cans black beans
  • 1 can of whole kernel corn
  • 12oz sliced white mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp. low fat shredded cheddar jack cheese -- per serving
  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tsp. sriracha sauce -- per serving

Steps

  1. Start the rice in a rice cooker approximately 20 minutes before beginning the other steps. Fill the rice cooker with water to the 2.5-cup line, or adjust according to your preference.
  2. Add olive oil to a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Once heated, add the onions, season with salt and pepper, and saut√© for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook for 3-4 minutes, until most of their water has evaporated.
  4. Lower the heat to medium-low. Add the drained chickpeas, corn, and black beans. Add the chili powder and paprika, and cook for another 5-8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once everything is heated through, remove from the heat and set aside.
  6. Evenly divide the rice among six meal-prep containers.
  7. Spoon the vegetable mixture evenly over the rice in each container.
  8. Store in the refrigerator for up to three days. To reheat, microwave on high for 2 minutes and 45 seconds, stirring after two minutes. Let it stand for a minute before serving. Top with cheese and sriracha, if desired.
