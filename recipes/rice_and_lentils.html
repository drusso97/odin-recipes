Rice and Lentils from a Rice Cooker

This delicious and healthy rice recipe can be used as a base or served as a side. Packed full of protein and healthy carbs, this rice is perfect for any diet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1/2 cup brown lentils
  • 1/2 cup pearled barley
  • 2 tbsp. cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 of vegetable stock
  • water as needed

Steps

  1. Rinse rice, lentils, and barley thoroughly and mix in a rice cooker pot; add olive oil, garlic, and stock concentrate and stir.
  2. Pour enough water over the mixture to reach the 2-1/2-cup mark on the rice cooker.
  3. 3. Press Start and let cook until the rice and lentils are tender, about 30 minutes.

Macro-nutrient info per serving:

Makes 3 servings

Calories 631
Protein 18g
Carbohydrates 122g
Fat 11g
