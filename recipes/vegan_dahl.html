High Protein Vegan Dahl with Lentils and Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. coconut oil
  • 2 brown onions (finely chopped)
  • 1 2/3 cups canned chickpeas
  • 1 head broccoli (cut into florets)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 4 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp. turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp. medium curry powder
  • 3/4 cup canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup puy lentils
  • 1 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 cup basmati or long-grain white rice
  • Half lemon (juice)

Steps

  1. Add the coconut oil into a large saucepan over a medium heat. Once melted, add the onions and fry for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add the drained chickpeas and broccoli and brown for 3-4 minutes. In a separate bowl, dissolve the stock cube in two cups of boiling water. Set aside.
  3. Add the garlic, turmeric, cumin, and curry powder to the frying pan and cook for a further minute while stirring. Pour the stock into the saucepan along with the chopped tomatoes.
  4. Add the lentils and black pepper, stir, and turn the heat low. Cover and simmer for 30-35 minutes, until the lentils are tender but still have a little bite.
  5. While waiting for the lentils, cook the rice according to the packet instructions.
  6. Once the lentils are cooked, squeeze in lemon juice and divide the dahl between meal prep containers alongside a portion of rice.
  7. Store in the refrigerator for up to three days. To reheat, microwave for four minutes on high, stirring once after three minutes. Allow to stand for a minute before serving.

Macro-nutrient info per serving:

Makes 3 servings

Calories 504
Protein 28g
Carbohydrates 83g
Fat 6g
